How should the exterior and interior connect?

This affects how the 3D scene is structured

A

One continuous scene

The exterior and AI room are built in the same 3D scene. The glasshus walls are literally the walls of the AI room — glass on the outside, interior decor on the inside. Player walks seamlessly from outside to inside. No loading, no transition.

Pros

  • Seamless experience
  • Can look out through glass from inside
  • Most realistic

Cons

  • More complex — exterior + interior in one scene
  • AI room walls need reworking
B

Scene transition at the door

Exterior is its own scene. When the player clicks/walks through the glass doors, a brief transition (fade/dissolve) takes them into the AI room. Similar to how the portal works for the nature room.

Pros

  • Simpler — exterior and interior are independent
  • Follows existing transition pattern
  • AI room stays mostly unchanged

Cons

  • Break in immersion at door
  • Can't see outside from AI room
