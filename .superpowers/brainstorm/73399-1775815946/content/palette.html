Which cozy indie palette?

Three directions — all warm, all playful. Pick the one that feels right.

WARM EARTHY WOOD

A — Stardew / Farm Cozy

Dark warm browns, honey golds, sage greens. Sunlit wood floor, parchment walls, amber lighting. Feels like a cosy cabin at golden hour.

SOFT PASTEL MAGIC

B — Pastel Dream ⭐ Recommended

Deep indigo dark, lilac & mint pastels, warm gold accents. Soft lavender walls, teal arcade screens, glowing candy-coloured buttons. A Short Hike meets Dream Daddy.

OCEAN NIGHT MAGIC

C — Spiritfarer / Twilight

Deep ocean navy, teal glows, warm coral & amber accents. Moonlit atmosphere with warm pops of colour. Slightly more dramatic — emotional cozy.
